# Avala Health Proof Story

Reference: `docs/00_SOURCE_OF_TRUTH.md`.

## Story

Health proof work demonstrates how AvalaOS governance can support a specialized proof vertical without merging that vertical into the core platform.

## Flow

1. Identify a Health proof process.
2. Map evidence, owner, risk, and approval needs.
3. Use AvalaOS Core to show assessment, governance, evidence, and delivery handoff patterns.
4. Keep Health implementation and naming separate.

## Boundary

This is a proof story only. It does not modify Health files, rename Health, or make regulated compliance claims.
