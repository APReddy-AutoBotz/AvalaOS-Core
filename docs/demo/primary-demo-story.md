# Primary Demo Story

Reference: `docs/00_SOURCE_OF_TRUTH.md`.

## Story

A business team wants to evaluate a process before investing in automation.

## Flow

1. Process Intake records volume, effort, systems, pain points, constraints, and evidence.
2. Deterministic Assessment calculates fit, value, risk, and recommendation outputs.
3. Recommended Operating Model explains why the process fits or does not fit AI, RPA, workflow, HITL, or agentic delivery.
4. Avala Studio creates editable delivery-ready docs from approved source data.
5. Avala Govern Card records owner, autonomy, risk, allowed actions, blocked actions, approval, evidence, and audit status.
6. Approval/Evidence Rules identify what must be reviewed before handoff.
7. Delivery Pack creates governed work items with source lineage.
8. Monitor shows portfolio value, risk, blockers, and decision status.

## Proof Point

The demo proves governed delivery: evaluate first, govern next, then hand off with evidence.
