## RLS Design Principles Summary

- Deny by default.
- Use `auth.uid()` as the identity anchor.
- Require active organization membership for tenant records.
- Require active workspace membership for workspace-scoped records after workspace schema exists.
- Require explicit role and permission gates for privileged reads and mutations.
- Deny anonymous tenant-data access.
- Deny cross-org and cross-workspace reads or writes unless AP approves a specific rule.
- Keep service-role access server-only with actor, correlation, and audit context.
- Keep service-role keys and provider secrets out of browser code and tenant-visible records.
