## Constraints And Indexes Summary

- `profiles`: `id` references `auth.users(id)` with cascade delete; active/non-deleted unique lower email; status/deleted lookup index.
- `organizations`: tenant root with active/non-deleted unique lower slug; status/deleted lookup index.
- `workspaces`: organization child with active/non-deleted unique lower slug per organization; unique `(id, org_id)` helper key; organization/status/deleted lookup index.
- `roles`: scoped system, organization, or workspace role authority with JSONB permissions retained; active unique slug indexes by scope; status/deleted lookup index.
- `organization_members`: organization membership authority with active/non-deleted unique `(org_id, user_id)`; organization/status and user/status indexes.
- `workspace_memberships`: workspace membership authority with composite workspace/org FK; active/non-deleted unique `(workspace_id, user_id)`; non-unique `(org_id, user_id)` index; workspace/status and user/status indexes.
- Status and scope checks are intentionally broad enough to support future lifecycle states while still rejecting unclassified authority states.

## RLS Enabled With No Policies
