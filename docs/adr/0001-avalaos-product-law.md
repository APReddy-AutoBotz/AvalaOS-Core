# ADR 0001: AvalaOS Product Law

## Status

Accepted.

## Context

Future work needs one operating law for scope, governance, and evidence decisions.

## Decision

AvalaOS follows: Assess before automation. Govern before execution. Humans approve risk. Evidence proves every decision.

## Consequences

Milestones must show how they preserve assessment, governance, approval, and evidence boundaries.

## Non-Goals

This ADR does not add runtime features or approval systems.

## Verification Impact

Evidence reviews must check source-of-truth alignment and scope boundaries.
