# AvalaOS Core MVP Roadmap

## Phase 1: Brand And Govern Lite

- Migrate visible product branding to AvalaOS Core.
- Align README, metadata, app shell, docs, and export titles.
- Add Avala Govern Lite card in the Decision Pack or Handoff Pack flow.
- Preserve deterministic scoring behavior and scoring regression coverage.

## Phase 2: Pilot Readiness

- Move supported AI document generation and refinement paths fully server-side.
- Harden BYOK/key reference handling and provider audit logging.
- Finalize Supabase deployment, RLS checks, private storage, and export flows.
- Expand evidence and approval audit trails.

## Phase 3: Governed Delivery Loop

- Improve handoff lineage from Avala Assess to Avala Studio to Avala Delivery Lite.
- Keep Delivery Lite focused on approved work items, owners, status, blockers, and lineage.
- Add clearer portfolio value/risk rollups in Avala Monitor.

## Phase 4: Enterprise Controls

- Add deeper admin controls, audit views, and security configuration.
- Expand govern cards into richer policy objects after the lightweight model proves useful.
- Avoid unsupported compliance claims unless independently verified and formally supported.
