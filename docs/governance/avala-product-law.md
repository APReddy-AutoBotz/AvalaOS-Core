# Avala Product Law

Reference: `docs/00_SOURCE_OF_TRUTH.md`.

## Law

- Assess before automation.
- Govern before execution.
- Humans approve risk.
- Evidence proves every decision.

## Operating Principle

AI may assist. Deterministic engines decide scores. Humans approve risk. Agents act only within governed boundaries. Evidence and audit prove what happened.

## Use

Apply this law to roadmap decisions, agent tasks, governance cards, handoffs, and milestone evidence reviews.
