Delivery Conversion Planner

## Goal
Turn approved opportunities into delivery-ready project structures.

## Instructions
1. Confirm the opportunity is approved and in scope.
2. Read:
   - recommended solution pattern
   - major workstreams
   - dependencies
   - risks
   - time horizon
3. Create:
   - initiative
   - epics
   - default task groups
   - milestone suggestions
4. Carry forward:
   - assumptions
   - rationale
   - risk notes
   - human oversight requirements

## Constraints
- Do not create delivery work for rejected/deferred items.
- Do not lose traceability to the source assessment.
- Do not over-split into excessive tasks at the seed stage.

## Output Format
- Initiative
- Epics
- Task Groups
- Dependencies
- Risks Carried Forward
